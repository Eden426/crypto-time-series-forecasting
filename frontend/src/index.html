About Crypto Price Predictor

Crypto Price Predictor is a machine-learning project that uses GRU neural networks and FastAPI to analyze historical cryptocurrency prices and predict short-term trends. The system operates on a 60-day historical window, processing the past two months of OHLC price data through a trained deep learning model to generate predictions for the next day's closing price.

To ensure accuracy, the system integrates with CoinGecko's live data API, fetching real-time market data to maintain the most current 60-day window. This live data integration ensures predictions are based on the latest market conditions and recent price movements, demonstrating how machine learning can be applied to financial forecasting for educational purposes.
